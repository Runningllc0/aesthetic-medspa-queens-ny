Frequently Asked Questions

What are lip fillers and how do they work?

Lip fillers are injectable dermal fillers made with hyaluronic acid, a substance naturally found in the body. They are used to add volume, improve shape, enhance symmetry, and define the lips. The treatment is non-surgical, minimally invasive, and provides immediate results with little downtime. Lip fillers are customizable, allowing your provider to create subtle or more noticeable enhancements based on your goals.

How long do lip filler results last?

Lip filler results typically last between 6 to 12 months, depending on the type of filler used, your metabolism, and lifestyle factors. Over time, the body naturally absorbs the filler. Many clients choose maintenance treatments to preserve their desired look and keep their lips looking full and refreshed year-round.

Is Botox safe for facial wrinkle treatment?

Yes, Botox is FDA-approved and widely used for reducing fine lines and wrinkles when administered by trained professionals. It works by relaxing targeted facial muscles that cause expression lines. Botox treatments are quick, safe, and effective, with minimal discomfort and no significant downtime when performed in a medical spa setting.

Is there downtime after lip fillers or Botox?

Most clients experience little to no downtime after lip fillers or Botox. Mild swelling, redness, or tenderness may occur and usually resolves within a few days. You can typically return to normal activities the same day, although it’s recommended to avoid strenuous exercise and excessive heat for 24 hours after treatment.

How do I know which aesthetic treatment is right for me?

The best way to determine the right treatment is through a professional consultation. During this visit, your provider will assess your facial structure, discuss your concerns, and recommend a personalized treatment plan. This ensures safe, effective results that align with your aesthetic goals and expectations.
